Citizenry Supports Educators

We're committed to supporting educators and community organizations that help students prepare for the US citizenship test. Whether you teach a citizenship class, run a community program, or work with immigrants and newcomers, we want to make it easier for your students to access high-quality practice tools.

Bulk Licenses for Your Students

Interested in purchasing bulk licenses for your class or organization? We offer options for educators and institutions. Get in touch and we'll work with you to find a solution that fits your needs.

Email us at:

support@citizenryapp.com

In your inquiry, please include your organization name, the number of students in your class, the class start date, and the class duration.

Why Citizenry for the Classroom?

  • Unlimited mock interviews with speech recognition. Students can practice as much as they need.
  • Full coverage of both the 2008 (100 questions) and 2025 (128 questions) USCIS civics tests
  • Ad-free experience with no distractions for your students
  • 20+ languages so students can study in the language they're most comfortable with
