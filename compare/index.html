Choosing the Right Citizenship Test App

Preparing for your US citizenship test is an important step on your path to becoming an American citizen. The right app can make the difference between feeling confident on test day and feeling unprepared. We've created detailed comparisons to help you make an informed decision.

What to Look for in a Citizenship Test App

  • Mock interviews to practice speaking (the test is oral, not written!)
  • Ad-free experience for distraction-free studying
  • Support for both 2008 and 2025 civics test versions
  • Multiple study modes (flashcards, quizzes, audiobooks)
  • Multi-language support for non-native English speakers
